Employee Survival Guide
Fazbear Entertainment is not responsible for damage to property or person.

Welcome to your new summer job at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your primary duty is to monitor the security cameras and ensure nothing goes wrong after hours. You have a very limited power supply for the building. Using the monitor, turning on the hall lights, and closing the security doors will drain your power. If you run out of power, everything goes dark.

Survive your shift from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. To help you survive the week, here is a detailed breakdown of the animatronic behavior patterns.

Bonnie the Bunny
  • Active Nights: Highly active starting Night 1. He is usually the first to leave the Show Stage.
  • Behavior: Bonnie will always approach your office from the West Hall (Left Side). He moves randomly and frequently between rooms.
  • How to Defend: Periodically check your Left Hall Light. If you see him standing in the doorway, close the Left Door immediately and wait for him to leave (check the light again to see his shadow).
Chica the Chicken
  • Active Nights: Active starting Night 1, usually moving shortly after Bonnie.
  • Behavior: Chica will always approach your office from the East Hall (Right Side). She tends to linger in rooms longer than Bonnie. If she is in the Kitchen (Cam 6), the camera will be disabled, but you can hear pots and pans clanking.
  • How to Defend: Check your Right Hall Light. If she appears in the window, close the Right Door until she wanders away.
Foxy the Pirate Fox
  • Active Nights: Becomes active on Night 2 (or Night 1 if you neglect the cameras entirely).
  • Behavior: Foxy hides in Pirate Cove (Cam 1C). He requires constant, brief check-ins. If you check him too little, or too much, he will slowly emerge from his curtain.
  • How to Defend: Keep a regular eye on Cam 1C. If the curtain is completely wide open and Foxy is missing, close your Left Door immediately, THEN check the West Hall (Cam 2A) to see him sprint down the hall. If he hits your closed door, you will lose a chunk of power.
Freddy Fazbear
  • Active Nights: Freddy is mostly inactive until Night 3. He becomes highly aggressive on Nights 4 and 5.
  • Behavior: Freddy sneaks entirely in the dark. He is very hard to see on cameras - you can usually only spot his glowing eyes. Unlike Bonnie and Chica, Freddy will NOT appear in your door lights. He attacks from the Right Side.
  • How to Defend: You must track his movement via cameras. When he reaches the East Hall Corner (Cam 4B), you must close the Right Door whenever you look at the monitor, or else he will sneak in and end your run.
